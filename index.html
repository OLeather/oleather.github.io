About Me

Hi, my name is Owen Leather. I enjoy computer science, mechanical engineering, woodworking, and playing guitar.

I have participated in the FIRST Robotics Competition for 3 years. I am now a mentor for FRC Team 1351.

I currently work as a software engineering intern at iMetalx.
