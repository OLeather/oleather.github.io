Story:

I participated in an internship at SwimClips in which I worked with two other students, FRC Team 1351 Mentor Mr. Fairley, and SwimClips Founder Mr. Griswold to design and manufacture a low profile camera gimbal. I contributed to the CAD design and assembly of the mechanism.


The design was specifically challenging because the gimbal needed to fit in a low profile underwater case, and we had to work with very tight tolerances. We went through many design iterations using 3D printed parts and manufactured the final product out of aluminum on a CNC milling machine.
